Keep the ocean clean forever.

The time for change is now.

For decades we have polluted our oceans, unaware of the consequences that we would later face. Decades of destruction now require decades of repair as the world begins to understand the impact of our tendencies.

An ongoing effort

Currently, there are several organizations dedicated to cleaning our oceans:

  • Surfrider Foundation:
    • Surfrider's powerful grassroots network works to protect coasts and oceans by supporting water quality testing, community partnerships, beach cleanups, and more.
  • Ocean Conservancy:
    • The conservancy's current programs include supporting sustainable fisheries, working to combat ocean acidification, and restoring the Gulf of Mexico.
  • Oceana:
    • Since 2001, Oceana has advocated for policy change using science-backed platforms in fishery management, clean energy, and much more.
  • Lonely Whale Foundation:
    • The Foundation has started the #StopSucking campaign to educate people about the perils of one-use plastic straws in the ocean and ultimately to stop using them.

Terms you should know

Educating yourself is the first step in protecting our oceans:

Capture
Catch large amounts of plastic within marine environments.
Accumulation
Properly store captured materials in the most efficient way.
Extraction
Extract waste from the worksite.
Recycling
Make use of materials. Catch, rinse, recycle and repeat - until the oceans are clean.
